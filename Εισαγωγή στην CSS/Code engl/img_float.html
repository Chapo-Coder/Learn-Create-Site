Aim of the department is to promote information science, computer systems and telecommunications, as well as their applications. The Department has modern facilities on the campus of the historic city of Arta.
The Department of Informatics and Telecommunications was founded in 2018 as part of the School of the University of Ioannina. Its main mission is to cultivate Computer Science, Networking of Computer Systems and Telecommunications, and their various applications. The Department has a Postgraduate and PhD degree.
